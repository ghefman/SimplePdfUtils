# Simple Pdf Utils

A fully on-device PDF editor for iPhone. Merge PDFs, photos, and Word documents; reorder or remove pages; add text; rotate pages; compress heavy pages — without uploading anything.

## What it does

- **Combine** PDFs, photos, and Word documents (.docx) into one file
- **Reorder** pages by dragging; swipe a row to mark it for removal
- **Add text** to any page — pick font, size, and colour; drag to reposition
- **Rotate** any page 90° clockwise with one tap
- **Preview** any page full-screen with pinch to zoom
- **Compress** heavy pages (over 300 KB) down to under 250 KB with one tap
- **Combine photos** into a grid layout on a single page
- **Save** via the iOS share sheet — you choose where the file goes

## Private by design

The app contains no networking code. Every feature works in Airplane Mode. You can verify this yourself.

- No accounts or sign-up
- No data collection, analytics, or tracking
- No ads, no subscriptions, no upsells
